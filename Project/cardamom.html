Cardamom [Ellettaria cardamomum] – Hindi name – Chotti Elaichi and English name – Lesser Cardamomum

Mainly grown in south india. green in colour, elaichi is coolant and are used as flavouring. Also provide relief from improper digestion, nausea and vomiting in pregnancy. Cardamom has anti-inflammatory and antibacterial property.

Part used: SEEDS

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

RASA – Katu [ pungent] , Madhura [ sweet]

GUNA [ QUALITIES] – Laghu , Ruksha

VIPAK – Katu

VEERYA – Sheet [ cold]

EFFECT ON Dosha: Balances vatta and kaffa

DOSAGE – 0.5 – 1gm

USES –

  1. Vomiting
  2. Diabetes
  3. Insomnia
  4. Cough
  5. Digestion
