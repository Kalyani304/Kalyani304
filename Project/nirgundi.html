NIRGUNDI [ VITEX NEGUNDO ]

Nirgundi is found throughout India. Nirgundi has been Used for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic activities.

PART – leaves, roots, fruits, and seeds , Leaf oil

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

Rasa : Katu, Kasaya, Tikta

Guna : Laghu [ light ]

Virya : Ushna [ hot]

Vipaka : Katu

Karma : Kesya,Sophahara, Visaghna,Smtriprada, Anulomna, vatta and kafa shamak

Effect on Dosha: Reduces Vata and Kapha Dosha but increases Pitta (in excess).

DOSAGE – Juice – 10-20 ml Powder – 1.5 – 3 gm

USES –

  1. Osteoarthritis
  2. Rheumatoid Arthritis
  3. Sciatica
  4. Joint disorders
  5. Spondylosis
