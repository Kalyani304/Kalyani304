BALA [ SIDA CORDIFOLIA]

Bala is used as a ingredient in massage oils which are used applied externally on arthritis, joint pain, paralysis and other Vata Roga. Analgesic (Pain relieving) , Anti-asthmatic (Treat or prevent asthma attacks). It is health tonic with rejuvenating, anti-inflammatory, and fat-burning properties and helps in fatigue. So in conclusion, Bala is one the best herb in our Ayurvedic herbs list.

Dosage- The dosage of dry root powder – 1 to 5 grams. The fresh juice dose – 3-6 teaspoon.

PART USED – In Ayurveda, leaves, seeds and roots are used for medicinal purpose.

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

  • Rasa(taste on the tongue): Tikta (Bitter), Madhura (Sweet)
  • Guna(Pharmacological Action): Guru (Heavy), Snigdha (Unctuous)
  • Virya(Action): Shita (Cooling)
  • Vipaka(transformed state after digestion): Madhura (Sweet)

EFFECT ON DOSHA – It balances tridosha i.e. Vata-Pitta-Kapha , It mainly clears high Pitta and Vata.

USES –

  1. URTICARIA
  2. PARALYSIS
  3. ARTHRITIS & RHEUMATISM
  4. BLEEDING PILES
  5. ULCERS, TUMOURS, BOILS, CYSTS
