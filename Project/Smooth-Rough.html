Smooth-Rough

Smooth (Shlakshana): predominantly composed of the Water element, increases the Pitta and Kapha Doshas and reduces the Vata Dosha. Foods that have a ‘smooth’ quality improves lubrication and flexibility within the body. Shlakshana also imbibes the quality for love, care and healing Substances with smooth quality include talcum powder, oil and avocado.

Rough (Khara): predominantly composed of the Air element, increases the Vata Dosha and reduces the Kapha and Pitta Doshas. Rough substances tend to reduce and deplete bodily tissue. Medicinally, ‘rough’ quality helps remove excess oiliness in the body however, excess dryness can cause dryness leading to dehydration and constipation. Foods having ‘rough’ quality include biscuits, raw vegetables, cabbage, apples, broccoli, artichoke, black beans, raw carrots, etc.
