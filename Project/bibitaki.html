BIBHITAKI [terminalia belerica]

It is an good remedy for catarrhal condition, congestion, bronchitis, asthma, and sore throats. A good tonic for eye. It is found throughout India. Bibhitaki one of the three fruits, in Ayurvedic preparation Triphala.

Part used – Fruit , seed, seed kernel.

Hindi name – bahera

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

RAS – KASAYA

GUNA – LAGHU, RUKSHA

VIRYA – USANA

VIPAKA – MADHURA

KARMA – KASHARA, KRIMINASNA,BHEDAKA, KESYA, KAPHAPITTAJ.

EFFECT ON DOSHA – It balances all the three doshas (vata, pitta, kapha) specially kapha dosha. As kapha helps to maintain overall body health and throat health.

Dosage: 3 – 6 grams

USES

  1. Rheumatid Arthritis
  2. Peptic ulcer, Mouth ulcer
  3. Fever
  4. Heart attacks
  5. Constipation
  6. Gastrointestinal anomalies
