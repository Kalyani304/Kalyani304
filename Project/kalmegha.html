KALMEGHA [Andrographis paniculata]

Next in our Ayurvedic herbs list is Kalmegha. It is also known as green chiretta. it is good for liver . kalmegha have properties like antibiotic, antinflammatory, antiviral, antibacterial. it is a good blood purifier. It is found throughout the plains and hills of tropical India, it is awell known ayurvedic drug for its bitter taste.

Part used: Whole plant

Dosage: Fresh juice 5-10 ml; Powder 1-3 grams

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

RASA – Tikt [ bitter]

GUNA [ quality] – Laghu , Ruksha

VIPAKA – Katu

VEERYA – Ushna

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Reduce kaffa and pitta

USES –

  1. Diarrhea
  2. General debility
  3. Fever
  4. Liver disorders
  5. Vomiting
  6. Blood purifier
  7. Eczema
