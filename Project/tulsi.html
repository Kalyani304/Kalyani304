TULASI [Ocimum sanctum]

Tulsi is one the best Ayurvedic herbs mentioned in our list which is found troughout India. Decrease pain and swelling (inflammation). Other chemicals might lower blood sugar in people with diabetes, Antioxidant, ANTICANCER Drug.

Dosage – Juice – 1-3ml powder form [seed] – 1-2 gm

PART USED – leaves, stems, and seeds .

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

Rasa – Tikta (Bitter), Kashaya (Astringent)

Guna – Laghu (Light), Ruksha (Dry), Tikshna (Sharp)

Veerya – Ushna (Hot)

Vipaka – Katu (Pungent)

Karma – Kapha, Vata , shamak

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Kapha-reducing, but it can also be used to pacify Vata and Pitta.

USES –

  1. Bronchitis
  2. Asthma
  3. Influenja
  4. Dyspepsia
  5. Hepatits
