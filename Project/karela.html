KARELA [ momordica charantia]

The next important herb in our Ayurvedic herbs list is Karela which is found throughout the india. Famous for its bitter flavour. Karela is a good blood purifier and laxative. Its bitter taste is due to presence of quinine. karela is dark green in colour. karela contains antioxidants, calcium , phosphorous, magnesium, iron and potassium. Karela is rich in vitamin A and vitamin C. Juice of leaves of bitter gourd is helpful in piles and alcoholism.
