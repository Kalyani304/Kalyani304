MARICH [PIPER NIGRUM]

The next herb in our Ayurvedic herbs list is Piper Nigrum which is also known as black pepper. black pepper has carminative action and used in flatulence, bloating, indigestion and gas. It lowers the cholesterol level. It is used in management of hyperthyroidism.

Part used : Fruits

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

  • Rasa(taste on the tongue): Katu (Pungent), Tikta (Bitter)
  • Guna(Pharmacological Action): Laghu (Light), Tikshna (Sharp), Ruksha (Dry)
  • Virya(Action): Ushna (Heating)
  • Vipaka(transformed state after digestion): Katu (Pungent)

EFFECT ON DOSHAS – It reduces Vata and Kapha but increases Pitta.

DOSAGE – The recommended dosage is Powder 250 mg to 1 gram.

USES –

  1. Chronic cold and cough
  2. Obesity
  3. Low appetite
  4. Hiccups
  5. Hoarseness
