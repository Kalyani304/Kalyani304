TRIPHALA

TRI – THREE , PHALA – FRUITS, that means it is a combination of three ayurvedic fruits , amalaki , bibhitaki, and haritaki. triphala balances and support all three dosha in the body. Triphala detoxifies the body and removes toxins. Triphala is also one of the best Ayurvedic herbs in our list. Triphala also helps boost your immunity, rich in vitamin E and C.

DOSAGE – 2-3 gm

THREE FRUITS-

AMLAKI – amla have antioxidant properties , Amla gives healthy and glowing skin but also improves eye sight. helps in weakness.

HARITAKI – Haritaki is rich in vitamin C and found to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. good cardiotonic and antiseptic.

BIBHITAKI – It is good for catarrhal condition, congestion, bronchitis, asthma, and sore throats. A good tonic for eye and a good rejuvenator. it is also having sedative action , and therefore used in insomnia.

EFFECT ON DOSHA – balances all the three dosha i.e vata , pitta, and kaffa .
