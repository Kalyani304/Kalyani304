NEEM [ Azadirachta indica ]

It is known for its bitter taste. The plant is found throughout India. Azadirachtin present in neem is the active insecticidal component of neem, Neem used against various infectious diseases. Neem leaves have good blood purifying property, therefore use in pimples, acne, and marks. Neem known for its Antiageing, antifungal ,anti inflammatory, antibacterial, rejuvenator, anti viral , detoxifying properties. Neem is also an important herb in Ayurvedic herbs list that contains the fatty acids, which also prevent wound healing.

PART USED – The bark, leaves, and seeds

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

RASA – tikta , kashaya

GUNA – laghu , ruksha

VEERYA- Sheet

VEEPAK – Katu

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Increase vatta, and balances pitta and kaffa.

DOSAGE – Neem leaves – 4 – 5 leaves

Neem churna – 1/4 – 1/2 tsf

Neem capsule – 1-2

USES –

  1. Gingivitis
  2. Diabetes
  3. Psoriasis
  4. Increase Immunity
  5. Piles
  6. Asthma
  7. Cough
  8. UTI
