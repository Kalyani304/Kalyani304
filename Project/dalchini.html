DALCHINI [ CINNAMOMUM ZEYLANICUM]

Cinnamon has carminative, antiseptic and astringent properties. Cinnamon is mainly used as aromatic spice with medicinal properties and it Lowers Blood Sugar Levels and has a Anti-Diabetic Effect. Dalchini is one of the herb in our Ayurvedic herb list that is loaded with anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

DOASAGE – Recommended dosage (1-3 grams)

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

  • Rasa[ on taste] : Madhura [ sweet ] , Katu (pungent), Tikta
  • Guna: Laghu (light), Ruksha (Dry), tikshna (sharp)
  • Veerya: Ushna (Hot)
  • Vipaka: Katu

EFFECT ON DOSHA – It increases Pitta but decreases vata and Kapha. It promotes digestion and cures indigestion.

PART USED – The inner bark of tree is collected and used as spice.

USES –

  1. Cold and Cough
  2. Diarrhea
  3. Arthritis pain
  4. Insomnia
  5. Weak Digestion
