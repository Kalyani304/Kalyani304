SHIGRU [Moringa Oleifera]

It is found in Himalayas of India, Middle East, Africa, America, and Philippines. It is mainly used as vegetable in India. Its leaves fight aganist malnutrition and diseases and also good for the eyes and vision. Shigru is one of the important herb in our Ayurvedic herbs list as it is an excellent source of iron. Its flowers helps the fertility in males by increasing sperm production. They have thermogenic, digestive, anti-spasmodic, anti-tumor, gas preventing, constipating, pain relieving, urine production stimulating, ophthalmic, cough relieving, anti-cancer, antibiotic . shigru is full of minerals, protein, vitamins, Carbohydrates, Proteins, Amino acid, fats and dietary fibre.

PART USED – Leaves , fruit , seed , flower , bark

Ayurvedic Properties –

1. RASA – KATU (Pungent) & TIKTA (Bitter)

2. GUNA – LAGHU (Light), RUKSHA (Dry), & TIKSHNA (Sharp)

3. VIRYA – USHNA (Hot)

4. VIPAKA – KATU (Pungent)

5. PRABHAVA – Anti-inflammatory

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Balances vata and kapha

Decoction – 10 – 15 ml
