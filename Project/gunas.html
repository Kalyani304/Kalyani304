Gunas In Ayurveda

Ayurveda considers that all substances are made of varying proportions of the five elements, and their composition reveals which qualities or Gunas they possess. This not only helps us better understand their physical nature but also helps us understand how to interact with the environment and everything around us. If a certain substance has a dominant Guna, consuming that substance increases that specific Guna is us and vice-versa. For example, consuming chillies which have the Gunas of ‘Hot’ and ‘Sharp’ would increase those qualities in us by consuming it. It is by understanding these qualities that we can also treat and manage health conditions in our body.

Hot - Cold

Dry - Oily

Smooth - Rough

Dense – Liquid

Soft – Hard

Stable – Moving

Gross – Subtle

Cloudy/Sticky – Clear
