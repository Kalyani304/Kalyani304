SHUNTHI [ zingiber officinale ]

The next herb in our list of Ayurvedic Herbs is Shunthi which is also known in Botany with the name of Zingiber officinale, is quite a recognized Ayurvedic herb. Drying of ginger after its processing with the help of lime water, Sonth is prepared. commonly used ingredient in Trikatu powder.

PART USED – Rhizome

PROPERTIES-

Guna : Laghu (Light), Tikshna (Sharp), Ruksha (Dry)

Virya : Ushna (Heating)

Vipaka: Madhura (Sweet)

EFFECT ON DOSHA – It helps to decreases Vata and Kapha doshas and increases Pitta dosha.

DOSE – 1-2 g of the drug in powder form

USES-

  1. Indigestion
  2. Ama dosha
  3. Inflammatory conditions
  4. Cough and cold
  5. Digestive disorders
  6. Dyspepsia
  7. Gas, flatulence
  8. Arthritis
