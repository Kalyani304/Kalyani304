BAEL [ aegle marmelos ]

Bale is one of the most important herb in Ayurveda. It is very popular in India , and offered to lord shiva. its fruit is is round, oval in shape. The outer cover of bael fruit is a very hard. bael contains tannins, steroid, phenols , vit c, b1, b2, copper, magnesium, iron etc. it is also helps in boosting immunity. bael have antioxidant, antiviral, anti diabetic , anti bacterial, mild laxative properties. it is also used in the treatment of numerous gut disease. it is found may parts of india, rajasthan.

PART USED – Leaves, Ripe and Unripe Fruits

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

RASA[ taste ] – Katu, Tikta, Kashaya

GUNA[ physical properties] – Grahi , Tikshna , Snigdha

VIRYA – Ushna [ hot]

VEEPAK – Katu [pungent]

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Balances vatta, pitta and kaffa

DOSAGE –

Bael Juice – ½-1 cup

Bael Churna – ¼-½ teaspoon twice a day.

Bael Candy – 4-5 candies

USES –

  1. Diarrhea
  2. Constipation,
  3. Bronchitis, Cough
  4. Asthma
  5. Obesity
  6. Heart disorders
  7. Anemia
  8. Osteoporosis
