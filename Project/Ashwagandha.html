ASHWAGANDHA [ withania somnifera ]

Next herb in our Ayruvedic Herbs list is Ashwagandha. Also known as Indian ginseng and winter cherry. Ashwagandha as a Rasayana that increase longevity and vitality. It is used to increase sexual capacity as an aphrodisiac. This herb reduces anxiety and stress, and helps fight aganist depression.

DOSE – Ashwagandha Powder is taken in a dose of 3-6 grams,

Part(s) used : Roots (mainly), leaves

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

Rasa (taste on the tongue): Kashaya (Astringent), Madhura (Sweet), Tikta (Bitter)

Guna (Pharmacological Action): Guru (Heavy)

Virya (Action): Ushna [Hot]

Vipaka (transformed state after digestion): Madhura (Sweet)

Effect on Dosha : Vata and Kapha Reducing, Pitta increasing

USES –

  1. Weight loss:
  2. White Discharge
  3. Rheumatism
  4. Inflamation
  5. Sexual Debility
  6. Thyroid
  7. Pain in joints
