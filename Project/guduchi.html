GUDUCHI [ TINOSPORA CORDIFOLIA ]

It is an antipyretic herb. It is found in large amount in India. Giloy is a very important, and extensively used Ayurvedic medicinal herbs, treat chronic cases of fever. It increases body immunity against diseases. It removes toxins from the body. It ia also having antioxidant and anit-ageing properties.

Part Used: Stem, Root, Plant, Fruit

Ayurvedic Properties –

  1. Rasa : Tikta, Kashaya
  2. Guna : Laghu [ light ]
  3. Virya : Ushna [ hot ]
  4. Vipaka : Madhura [ sweet ]

EFFECT ON DOSHA –

DOSAGE – 3-6 g in powder form, 20-30 g of the drug for decoction.

USES- Cough, Cramp

  1. Diabetes, Dysentery, Dyspepsia
  2. Erysipelas [ red patches on the skin] , diarrhea
  3. Fever, Fracture
  4. Gout
