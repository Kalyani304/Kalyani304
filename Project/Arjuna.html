ARJUNA [ TERMINALIA ARJUNA ]

PART USED – STEM [ BARK]

Ayurvedic Properties –

  • Rasa (Taste): kashaya/Astringent,
  • Guna (Characteristics): Ruksha/Dry
  • Virya (Potency): Sheet/ Cool,
  • Vipaka (Post Digestive Effect): Katu/Pungent

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Balances vatta, pitta and kaffa dosha

AYURVEDIC FORMULATION –

  1. Arjunarishta
  2. Prabhakarvati
  3. Pushyanuga churna

DOSAGE – Arjuna Powder – 2-3 g

USES –

  1. Diabetes
  2. Cardiac disease
  3. Hypertension
  4. UTI [ urinary tract infection]
  5. Weakness
  6. Obesity
  7. Leucorrhea
  8. Osteoporosis
