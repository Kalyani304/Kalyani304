HARIDRA [ CURCUMA LONGA]

Haridra is an Ayurvedic well known immunity booster herbs that effectively protects against infections. It possesses various effective properties such as anti-inflammatory, anti-allergic, antiseptic, blood cleansing etc.

Part(s) used : Rhizome, tubers

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

  1. Rasa (taste on the tongue): Katu (Pungent), Tikta (Bitter)
  2. Guna (Pharmacological Action): Ruksha (Dry)
  3. Virya: Ushna (Heating)
  4. Vipaka (transformed state after digestion): Katu (Pungent)

DOSE – the dose of turmeric powder for adults is 2 to 5 grams, and for children it is 1 to 2 grams.

USES –

  1. Vata Vyadhi problems – pain in joints, gout, joint swelling, morning stiffness, rheumatoid arthritis
  2. Bronchitis
  3. Kidney stone
  4. Diarrhea
  5. Diabetes
  6. Cancer
