SHALLAKI [ BOSWELLIA SERRATA]

Shallaki tree is native to India , mainly in punjab and maharashtra. the medicinal product of this tree is its resin, A single tree can produce about one to two kilograms of gum in a year. Shallaki extensively in the treatment of painful joint diseases and muscular pain. shallaki have anti-inflammatory properties.it improves brain function.

PART USED – Bark, resin [ gum]

RASA – Kashaya , Tikta , Madhur

GUNA – Laghu , Ruksha

VEERYA – Sheeta

VEEPAKA – Katu

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Balances pitta and kaffa

DOSAGE – Bark – 50 – 100 ml Gum – 1 – 3 gm

USES –

  1. Osteoarthritis
  2. Rheumatid arthritis
  3. Cancer
  4. Ulcerative colitis
  5. Cold and Flu
