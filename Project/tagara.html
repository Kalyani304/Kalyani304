TAGARA [ VALERIANA WALLICHII]

It is found in india, in himalayan region . Tagar is nervine, Anti inflammatory sedative,analgesic , antispasmodic, carminative, laxative, hypotensive, and aromatic. Tagar are used to treat insomnia and anxiety. It prevents gas formation , so it helps in abdominal pain and cramps. it also reduces effects of snake poisoning. local application of tagara is useful for reduce pain occurs from Bone Fracture and Joints affected with Rheumatoid Arthritis.

PART USED – Root

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

1. RASA (Taste) – TIKTA (Bitter), KATU (Pungent), KASAYA (Astringent)

2. GUNA (Main Quality) – LAGHU (Light), SNIGADH or SNEHA (Oily)

3. VIRYA (Potency) – USHNA (Hot)

4. VIPAKA (Result) – KATU (Pungent)

EFFECT ON DOSHA – Balances vatta and kapha dosha

DOSAGE – Root Powder – 250 to 1000 mg, Tablet – 1 tablet

USES –

  1. Fever
  2. Osteoarthritis
  3. Rheumatid arthritis
  4. Asthma
  5. Insomnia
  6. Hepatitis
  7. Hypertension
  8. Constipation
