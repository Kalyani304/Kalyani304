BRAHMI [ BACOPA MONNIERI]

Brahmi is called as medhya Rasayana (improves mental health, memory, Rasayana that prevents aging and gives longevity). Having Properties like Antioxidant, adaptogenic, astringent, diuretic, sedative .

Part used: Whole plant

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

Rasa: tikta and kashaya

Virya: sheeta [ cold]

Vipaka: madhura [ sweet ]

Prabhava/ special potency: alleviates epilepsy and hysteria

EFFECT ON Dosha: Tridosha mainly Kaphapittahara, can increase Vata in excess

DOSAGE – Brahmi Juice – 2-4 teaspoons once a day.

Brahmi Churna – ¼-½ teaspoon twice a day.

USES –

  1. Brain and nervine tonic
  2. Leprosy
  3. Epilepsy
  4. Anaemia
  5. Asthma
  6. Insanity
