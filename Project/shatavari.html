SHATAVRI [ASPARAGUS RACEMOSUS]

In ayurveda Shatavari is known as Rasayana that increases the fertility, longevity, treat weakness and prevents aging. shatavariis also have rejuvenating and nourishing effects.

PART USED – The root which has a long white tuber part of the plant is used.

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

Rasa (taste on the tongue): Madhura (Sweet), Tikt (Bitter)

Veerya – Sheet [ cold]

Veepak – Madhur

Guna – Guru , Snigdha

EFFECT ON DOSHA – balance vata and pitta (two of the three doshas)

DOSAGE – churn – 1/4 – 1/2 teaspoon juice – 2-3 ml

USES – Bronchitis

  • Anxiety
  • Diabetes
  • Diarrhea
  • Heartburn
  • Constipation
  • Premenstrual syndrome
