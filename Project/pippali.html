PIPPALI [ piper longum ]

The roots and fruits of Pippali are used for its medicinal purposes. Long pepper having properties like antioxidant, decongestant, expectorant, warming, analgesic.

PART USED – Fruit and root

RECOMMENDED DOSAGE OF PIPPALI

  • Churna – 4-8 pinch
  • Capsule – 1-2 capsules
  • Tablet – 1-2 tablet

AYURVEDIC PROPERTIES –

Rasa –Katu (Pungent)

Guna – Laghu (Light), Tikshna (Sharp)

Veerya- Sheeta (Cold)

Vipaka- Madhura (Sweet)

Karma –Kapha, Vata

DOSAGE – 0.5 – 1gm

EFFECTS ON DOSHA – aggravate the Pitta doshas and balance the Vata (air) doshas and Kapha doshas

USES

  1. ANEMIA
  2. INSOMNIA
  3. INDIGESTION
  4. LIVER PROBLEMS
  5. SPLEEN PROBLEM
